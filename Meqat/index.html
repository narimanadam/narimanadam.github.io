Our Philosophy

ميقات

ميقات تهدف الي مساعدة كل مسلم مدرك لقيمة الوقت ، ومؤمن بالقول الشهير

للحسن البصري

ي ابن آدم إنما أنت أيام، كلما ذهب يوم ذهب بعضك

و ذلك من خلال وسائل متعددة أهمها الأن منتجات متنوعة و مبتكرة

Miqat

aims to help Muslim Youth who are aware of the value of time, and who believe in the famous saying of

AlHassan AlBassry:

Son of Adam! Life is only days, so if your day passes, your life becomes shorter.

This help is provided through several innovative and different facilities and products.

Our Products

the first product:

notebook 2015

The Notebook of 2015, designed so that the user writes down his daily tasks as well as the main points of what he learned that day. It contains as well as reminders for the most important daily ‘Ebadat (Acts of worship) such as the five prayers, Qur’aan, fitness, charity. It’s also designed to follow up on a weekly basis the expenses of that week, a weekly shedule of your personal readings and to write down your most important ideas through the week. Another advantage of this product is that it introduces you daily to a new Hadith or a quote of the Salaf ul-Saleh.

Size: 90 Pages, A5, Hard cover

The book of ramadan:

Ramadan 1436

Ramadan product is designed so that the user assigns to exploit this blessed month optimal exploitation. The product is designed so that the user arranges his day in Ramadan based on the five daily prayers, he is reminded too with the daily ‘Ebadat ( acts of worship) and the Sunnan for Ramadan starting with the prayers, charity, qur’aan to kinship, taraweeh and Du’aa. It encourages the user also to write and record daily thoughts and interpretations of the most important lessons he learned from his daily readings of the Qur'an in Ramadan.

Size: 40 Pages, A5, Soft cover

The booklet of dhul hiijah:

dhul hiijah 1436

Small booklet, simple to use. The user is reminded with the importance of the first ten days of Dhul Hijjah. For it was narrated from Ibn ‘Abbas (may Allah be pleased with him) that the Prophet (sallaAllahu ‘alayhi wasallam) said: “There are no days on which righteous deeds are more beloved to Allah than these ten days.” They said: “Not even jihad for the sake of Allah?” He said: “Not even jihad for the sake of Allah, unless a man goes out himself for jihad taking his wealth with him and does not come back with anything.” (Reported by Bukhari) Designed from the inside to remind the user with the Fara’ed (Obligatory acts of worship) and various examples of good deeds, as well as abandoned Sunnan in order to revive them.

Size: 25 Pages, A6, Soft cover
